URL Pre-Filter

✕ Skip (bukan vendor)

  • youtube.com, tiktok.com
  • reddit.com, quora.com
  • medium.com, blogspot.com
  • wikipedia.org
  • tribunnews.com, kompas.com, detik.com
  • instagram.com, facebook.com
  • linkedin.com/posts (bukan company)

✓ Keep (likely vendor)

  • *.co.id, *.com company domains
  • indotrading.com, ralali.com
  • tokopedia.com/shop/*
  • URLs with /products, /pricing, /contact
  • alibaba.com listings
  • bukalapak.com/u/*
  • URLs matching company name patterns
